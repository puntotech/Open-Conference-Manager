Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

  1. Go to Your Facebook Account’s Setting & Privacy. Click ”Settings“.
  2. Then, go to ”Apps and Websites” where you will see all of the apps and websites that your Facebook account is connected to.
  3. Select the option box of Open Conference Manager.
  4. Click the remove button.
  5. Congratulations, you've successfully deleted your Facebook data.
